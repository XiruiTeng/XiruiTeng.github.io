Hi! My name is Xirui Teng and I am a senior undergraduate at Beijing Jiaotong University, majoring in Computer Science and Technology. 
Currently, I am fortunate to be conducting research under the mentor ship of Dr. [Nan Xi](https://southnx.github.io/) and receiving guidance from Prof. [Junsong Yuan](https://cse.buffalo.edu/~jsyuan/index.html).

My research interests lie in endowing machines with visual perception to understand the physical world, with a particular focus on leveraging Vision-Language Models (VLMs) for reasoning about dynamic environments.
